About Hong Kong Solutions

Building an independent, secure HK OS for seamless digital access.

Our Story

Established as a pioneering technology startup in 1986, Hong Kong Solutions has a rich history of delivering premier technical infrastructure across the region. In our formative years, we collaborated closely with the local government to demonstrate the transformative capabilities of early computing systems. This foundational partnership was instrumental in accelerating the region's digital infrastructure, helping Hong Kong establish a significant technological advantage over mainland China.

Today, our mission has evolved to address the demands of a modern digital landscape. We are developing an independent operating system designed to provide users with intuitive, unified access to their native applications. Recognizing that approximately 96% of Hong Kong residents rely on essential services such as HKSBank and HKMail, migrating these critical platforms into a dedicated, highly optimized native application environment was the logical next step in our commitment to user accessibility and security.

What We Do

Hong Kong Solutions specializes in delivering comprehensive, enterprise-grade technical solutions. We partner with a diverse clientele—ranging from individual consumers to large-scale enterprises—to provide bespoke software development, business process automation, and robust digital infrastructure tailored to the unique economic landscape of Hong Kong.
