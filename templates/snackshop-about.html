A little shop that grew up

SnackShop started life as a corner-shop idea in the UK — somewhere friendly to duck into for a snack and a chat. Fifteen years on, that same warmth now reaches customers right around the world.

🧺 Our Story

"We wanted SnackShop to feel less like a checkout, and more like popping round to a shop that knows you."

'11
A small shop, a simple idea
SnackShop opened its doors in 2011 as a modest convenience snack shop in the UK, stocking everyday treats and local favourites for a community of regulars. From the very start, the goal was simple: good snacks, fair prices, and a friendly face behind the counter.
'15
Finding our footing
Word of mouth turned a single shop into a small but loyal following. We leaned into what made us different — proper care over product choices, and treating every customer like they'd been coming in for years.
'19
Taking the leap online Growth
With demand growing beyond our four walls, SnackShop moved online — bringing the same hand-picked selection and homely service to customers who couldn't just pop in.
'26
Hello, Hong Kong Worldwide
In 2026, SnackShop was introduced to customers in Hong Kong — our first proper step onto the world stage, and a moment we're enormously proud of. Same cosy shop, new neighbours.
🏆
Our biggest milestone yet 2026
That same year, SnackShop was welcomed on as a native app on the operating system itself — built right in, rather than just bolted on. It's the achievement we're proudest of to date, and one that still feels a little surreal for a shop that started with a single till and a stack of crisps.
🍪 From one shop in the UK to a native app used by customers across the world — thank you for growing with us. Here's to the next chapter.
