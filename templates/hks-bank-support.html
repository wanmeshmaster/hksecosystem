Personal Customers/Customer Service for HKS Bank

HKS Bank Customer Service

We've put together shortcuts to help you handle your banking matters quickly online. Choose a topic below, or contact us directly and we'll get back to you.

You can also reach us anytime by emailing hksbank.support@hkmail.cn. Messages sent from the form below are logged as a support case so we can track it through to resolution.

Log in to send a message to customer service.

Login & Security

Contact customer service using the form above and we'll help verify your identity and get you back into your account.

From the homepage, select "Create an account" and fill in your details. You'll need an HKMail address on hand — we'll send a confirmation code there to verify it's you.

Your account is protected by your username and password. If you ever notice activity you don't recognize, contact customer service right away.

Accounts

From your dashboard, look for the option to open a new account alongside your primary account. It'll appear in your accounts list right away.

Your balance is shown on each account card on your dashboard. Select "View History" on any account to see its full transaction history.

Not yet — this is something we're planning to add. In the meantime, additional accounts are labelled in the order you opened them.

Cards

Open the Cards section on your dashboard and select the card in question — you can freeze it instantly from there.

On your dashboard's Cards section, select the card to reveal its full number, expiry, and CVV whenever you need them.

Contact customer service with your request and the account it should be linked to, and we'll take it from there.

Transfers

Use "Send Money" on your dashboard, enter the recipient's username and an amount, and confirm — it's instant.

Use "Transfer Money" on your dashboard to move funds between any of your own accounts.

Contact customer service with the date, amount, and account involved, and we'll look into it for you.
